Multi-Line Plots

These plots typically show how more than one variable react over time to the stimulus of another variable, in this case time. The representations can be made in a number of ways, but it is necessary to keep proper scales of axes to mitigate results being misinterpreted.

Small Multiples- is a collection of plots at various intervals showing the reaction in more detail.

Polar Coordinates- can be interesting if a single time period is used. Try to scale the Year Range to a single year and you can track the progress of variables over time radially.
