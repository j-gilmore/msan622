The Data: Road Casualties in Great Britain, 1969–84

UKDriverDeaths is a time series giving the monthly totals of car drivers in Great Britain killed or seriously injured Jan 1969 to Dec 1984. Compulsory wearing of seat belts was introduced on 31 Jan 1983. Seatbelts is more information on the same problem.

Instead of describing the entire dataset, below are the three variables of interest for this visualization.

Drivers- car drivers killed.

Front- front-seat passengers killed or seriously injured.

Rear- rear-seat passengers killed or seriously injured.

Source

Harvey, A.C. (1989) Forecasting, Structural Time Series Models and the Kalman Filter. Cambridge University Press, pp. 519–523. Durbin, J. and Koopman, S. J. (2001) Time Series Analysis by State Space Methods. Oxford University Press.
