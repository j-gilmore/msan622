Variable Definitions

GDP Percentil Range- Set the percentile of GDP range. Plots will adjust provided there is supporting data.

Population- State population estimate as of July 1, 1975

Income- State per capita income (1974)

Illiteracy- State illiteracy rate as a percentage of population (1970)

Murder- Murder and non-negligent manslaughter rate per state 100,000 population (1976)

HS Grad- State percent of high-school graduates (1970)

Frost- Mean number of days with minimum temperature below freezing (1931-1960) in capital or large city

Pop.Density- State population/state area

State Perc of Nat Pop- Percentage of national population

State Inc. of GDP- State income as a percentage of national GDP

State Inc. of Reg- State income as a percentage of region GDP
