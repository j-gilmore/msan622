About Word Stems

In order to make a more in-depth analysis of any corpus of text, word stems are used. Word stems are the root of a word and reduce redundency within a corpus. For example, the word 'resolve' may have many endings such as resolves, resolved or resolving. By using the word stem, we can evaluate one root word instead of three words that have the same meaning.

This visualization evaluates the frequency of word stems within The Divine Comedy.
